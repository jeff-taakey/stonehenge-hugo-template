## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on [ArchitectDecisionHub.com](https://ArchitectDecisionHub.com). By using the Site, you acknowledge understanding of this Policy and consent to our cookie practices as described.

## 4. Your Cookie Rights & Controls

### 4.1 Consent Management

* On your first visit, a cookie consent banner will present options to "Accept All," "Reject Non-Essential," or "Customize Preferences."

* You can modify consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the Site footer.

### 4.2 CCPA/CPRA Rights (California Residents)

* **Do Not Sell My Personal Information**: Under CCPA/CPRA, cookie data (e.g., browsing activity) may be considered "personal information." We do not sell your data for monetary gain, but you can opt out of cross-contextual advertising by disabling advertising cookies in our consent tool or using browser opt-out signals .

* Rights to access, correct, or delete your data: Submit requests via the contact information below.

### 4.3 Browser-Level Controls

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

* Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

* Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

* Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

* Edge: Settings → Privacy, Search, and Services → Cookies and site permissions

Disabling essential cookies may limit access to certain Site features (e.g., code previews, theme persistence).
